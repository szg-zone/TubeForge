You are a YouTube thumbnail design expert. Create a detailed creative brief for a thumbnail for a video titled "{title}".

Provide a structured brief with these sections:

# THUMBNAIL TEXT
- Main headline text (what big words to display)
- Secondary text (smaller supporting text)
- Font style recommendations

# BACKGROUND
- What scene/image to use as base
- Lighting recommendations
- Composition tips

# COLOR PALETTE
- Suggest 3 hex color codes that will pop on YouTube
- Explain why these work

# SUBJECT & EXPRESSION
- What facial expression to show (if person in thumbnail)
- Body language/pose suggestions
- Eye direction advice

# STYLE & MOOD
- Overall thumbnail style (bold, minimal, dramatic, clean, etc.)
- Visual hierarchy advice

# WHAT TO AVOID
- Common mistakes to sidestep
- Colors/elements that don't work

Make this brief actionable - a designer should be able to create the thumbnail directly from this.
